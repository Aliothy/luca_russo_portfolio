Hi, I’m Luca, a PhD student in Robotic Engineering at University of Illinois at Chicago, driven by a passion for robotics and aerospace innovation.

My academic journey began with a Bachelor’s in Aerospace Engineering and a Master’s in Mechatronics Engineering at Politecnico di Torino, Italy, where I honed my skills in designing and integrating complex systems. Today, my research focuses on heterogeneous robotic systems, developing collaboration between UAVs, UGVs and legged robots to tackle real-world challenges.

Beyond my work in robotics, I’m also a lifelong athlete, having wrestled since the age of 5. Wrestling has instilled in me a strong sense of discipline, perseverance, and strategic thinking—qualities that deeply influence my approach to engineering challenges.

Whether I’m designing a multi-robotic system or competing on the mat, I strive for precision, adaptability, and excellence. Feel free to explore my projects, connect with me, or join me on this exciting journey into the future of robotics.
