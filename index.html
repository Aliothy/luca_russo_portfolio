About Me

I’m an Aerospace and Mechatronics Engineer passionate about robotics and space. The ability to make a system move autonomously and make it aware of the surrounding environment has always amused me. It is why I pursued a master’s degree in Mechatronics Engineering where I gained the knowledge and skills necessary to design and build such systems. I am passionate about exploring the intersection of mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering to create innovative and practical solutions that can make a difference in the world. In my free time, I love sports, mainly because I have been a wrestler since I was a kid!
